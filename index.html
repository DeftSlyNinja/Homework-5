Homepage


This website helps you keep track of your backlog of video games and allows you to filter them by genre or search for them by name.

Backlog will display your list of games in order of priority.

Search / Filter will allow you to filter games by genre or search for them by name.

Add Game will allow you to add new games to your backlog.
